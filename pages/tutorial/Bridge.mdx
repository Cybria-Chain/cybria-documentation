# Bridge From ETH CYBA

### Bridge using Cybria Bridge

1. Navigate to https://bridge.cybria.io and connect your wallet to Ethereum.
2. Select currency: Cyba (To Chain will auto fill).
3. On your first bridge transaction, approve Cyba spending first. In Metamask, make sure to select Use Default as custom spending cap.
4. Enter the amount of Cyba to bridge (or press Max to select your entire balance).
5. Press Next and review your transaction before confirming with your wallet.
8. Wait for the transaction to complete, switch your wallet to Cybria Mainnet, your native coins will arrive soon!
